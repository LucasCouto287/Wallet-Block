Privacy & Security

WalletBlock is made to keep all the data secure and make sure no other party than yourself can access your information. All the sensitive data are completely encrypted and later than saved on the server to make it accessible to your multiple devices. The data decrypting can only take place on your local device, without any third party involved. To learn more about the high security standards and how we make sure, no one can access your transaction and metadata.
